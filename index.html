Hi! I am Eric Simon Larios.

I am an incoming 4th yr. college student from PUP Manila and currently enrolled under the Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering program. I am a flexible developer that learns fast and has a love-hate relationship with arrays.
BIO

Born on November 6, 2000 in a remote municipal town called Rodriguez, Rizal. He took his elementary education at Tagumpay Elementary School, a pseudo INC school because majority of the students and teachers, are INC. After he graduated elementary level, he was enrolled to Virgen Del Pillar School (VDPS), a catholic school that is managed by nuns, his experience there is, well, it was life-changing. He only studied in VDPS until his 9th grade and later on transferred to Kasiglahan Village National School (KVNHS) for the final year of his high school life. KVNHS reminds him of a prison due to the abundance of grilled windows and doors.

After he finished his junior high school, he enrolled to PUP Manila as a senior high school. He took up the Information and Communications Technology (ICT) strand because he want to gain some technical skills before pursuing his desired course, MedTech. SHS is the most fun stage of his life because he made so many friendships with similarity in interest. He graduated his SHS with honors and decided that he will pursue IT related courses, instead of his initial desired course, because he find programming enjoyable. He passed the college entrance exam of PUP and enrolled to the Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering program, which turns out the most regretable decision he has made in his life, because little did he know that this course is hellish to the core.
